Philbound is an online platform connecting real estate agents, brokers, and investors. It simplifies listing management and provides buyers and renters access to properties across the Philippines. With advanced real estate marketing tools, Philbound aims to streamline transactions for an organized and smooth experience.

Ang Philbound ay isang online platform na mag-uugnay sa mga real estate agents, brokers, at investors sa Pilipinas. Gamit ang advanced real estate marketing tools, layunin nitong gawing mas maayos at organisado ang pamamahala ng mga property listings at transaksyon para sa mas mabilis na bagbenta, pagbili, at pag renta ng mga real estate properties.
